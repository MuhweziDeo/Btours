About Us

Bridget Tours is a tourist company aimed at improving tourism in Africa and Worldwide Its Based in Uganda, Headquaters located in Jinja. It carries out tourist activities based on all the natural resources like game parks, water falls, historical sites, mountains etc..

It deals with various stake holders like hotels, transportation companies.
It was started in 2017 by K.Bridget in a small town in Jinja with the aim of improving and apprecaiting the beauty of Uganda's resources as the Pearl of Africa
